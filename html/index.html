Welcome to PPIA UNSW

Perhimpunan Pelajar Indonesia Australia (PPIA) UNSW is an organisation with the goal of strengthening the fraternity among Indonesian students at UNSW and introducing the diversity of Indonesian culture to other students on campus. We aim to create exciting events that are both fun and beneficial for members of our society!
